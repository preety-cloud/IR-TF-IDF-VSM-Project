Standardization Requirements of a National Program for Information Transfer .A Wigington, R.L. Wood, J.L. The authors of this article represent two specializations in the spectrum of information transfer activities, that of the computer and communication system engineer and that of the librarian.  These points of view are combined in examining the requirements for standardization in the national efforts to use automation in publication, library, abstracting and indexing, and information-retrieval activities.
