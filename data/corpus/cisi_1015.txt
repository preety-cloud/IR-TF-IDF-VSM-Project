Participative Management as Related to Personnel Development .A Marchant, M.P. Theory and practice regarding patterns of decision- making in libraries have been relatively neglected aspects of library administration.  Yet the decisions by which a library attempts to control its operations are of major importance to its welfare and effectiveness.
