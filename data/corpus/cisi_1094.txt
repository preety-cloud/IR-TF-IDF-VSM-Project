Improving the Work of a Central Bureau of Technical Information .A Shekhurin, D. E. The process of integration of sciences and the growing cooperation of creative efforts in the various fields of science and technology intensify the need for interdisciplinary information.. The prime task of a regional information agency is to meet the information wants of local enterprises.. An efficient fulfillment of that task presupposes a study of the demand for interdisciplinary information at the patron enterprises in order to set up an appropriate information and reference collection..
