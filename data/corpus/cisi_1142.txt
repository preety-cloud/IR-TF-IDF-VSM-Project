Science on science - Introduction to a general science of science .A Dobrov, G.M. This book generalizes world and soviet experience of science, gives original representation of science as informational process which allows one to use quantitative methods in scientometrics, analyzes extensive data on the experience of formulating scientific potential and organizing scientific work, formulates general principles of organization, management and disposition of modern scientific centers.
