Principles of Studying of Information Requests .A Popilova, L.L. Earlier publications on the study of information requests consisted of statistical listing of the most usable sources of information. Lately there is an increasing number of publications which consider the methods for studying information requests. Analysis of these publications shows that we can discern two basic tendencies: studying subfield groups of researchers and groups of researchers, classified by topical information sources.
