Issues in the informational analysis of documents. .A Polushkin, V.A. In scientific process and primary scientific documents there are objective characteristics, which allow the accurate and unambiguous reflection of the form and content of primary statements n the informational analysis of their secondary pattern. Using these characteristics allows one to develop a model of standardized bibliographic and abstract document description and minimize subjectivity in information analysis. The objective characteristics of form and content of documents and corresponding elements of bibliographic and abstractive description must be based on application of classification schemes.
