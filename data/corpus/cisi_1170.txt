Some Aspects of Subject Acquisition and Detailed Subject Retrieval of Patent Information .A Shenderov, V. Z. Aspects of subject acquisition and retrieval of patent information are discussed.. A patent-information service system is conventionally separated into two parts:  a subject acquisitions system designed for stock acquisition and search file building, and a detailed subject retrieval system designed to deal with specific user requests.. The performance of both systems is analyzed using patent classifications as examples..
