Basis of Scientific Information .A Mikhailov, A.I. The present monograph is one of the first attempts to expound the basis of scientific information, its theory, systems of methods and organization. The monograph formulates subject and method of scientific information theory; gives specification of different kinds of documents as sources of scientific information; sheds light on method and forms of analytical-synthetic document processing; expounds basic principles of information retrieval and ways to mechanize and automatize it.
