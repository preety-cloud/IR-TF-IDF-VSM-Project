Personnel Administration in Libraries .A Stebbins, K.B. Since 1958 when the first edition of the present work was issued, additional studies, research programs and experimentation have contributed to changes in personnel work and the large area of human relations.  The impact of these developments has been and should be felt in libraries as well as in industry, government and business.
