The Practice of Management .A Drucker, P.F. We have available today the knowledge and experience needed for the successful practice of management.  But there is probably no field of human endeavor where the always tremendous gap between the knowledge and performance of the leaders and the knowledge and performance of the average is wider or more intractable.  This book does not exclude from its aims the advancement of the frontier of knowledge; it hopes, indeed, to make some contribution to it.
