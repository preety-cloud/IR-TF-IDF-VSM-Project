Reader in Library Cooperation .A Reynolds, M.M. This volume is intended as a means of exploration for the practicing librarian and as a textbook for the library school student.  It will try to draw attention to significant social, behavioral, theoretical, organizational, functional, and operational generalizations about library interrelationships; and to suggest a sense of the total fabric of the cooperative endeavor.
