Citation Analysis .A Gouldsmit, S.A. The Science Citation Index is a valuable and powerful tool when used for the purpose for which it was intended, as an aid in literature search.  It also invites a variety of statistical investigations, which must, however, be considered with prudence, since they may lead to misleading results.  No matter how cautiously the authors express themselves, the casual readers,  that is the majority, will treat the results as established facts and forget about the assumptions underlying them.
