The Annual Review of Information Science and Technology .A Cuadra, C.A. .B 1967 This volume is the second in a series of Annual Reviews of progress in the field of Information Science and Technology.  Like its predecessor, it attempts to describe, compare, and evaluate the most significant work that has been reported in the field during the past year.  The effort has been undertaken in the belief that such taking stock of accomplishments provides a valuable service to the specialists in the information science field.
