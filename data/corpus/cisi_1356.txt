The Scientist Versus Machine Search Services: We are the Missing Link .A Maier, J.M. To take advantage of computerized data bases to improve their services to scientists without incurring prohibitive in-house expense, the librarians at the  Boulder Laboratories have campaigned to increase awareness and utilization via personal interviews, seminars, surveys, and critiques.  Data bases most studied were DDC, NASA, SIE, ASCA, and the University of Georgia.
