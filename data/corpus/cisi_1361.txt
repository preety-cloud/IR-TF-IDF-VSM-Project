User Needs and Their Effect on Information Center Administration A Review 1953/66 .A Coover, R.W. A determination of the needs of users is absolutely essential to the management of an Information Center.  Various techniques of doing this (e.g., interview, diary, questionnaire, etc.) are detailed along with their limitations. Some specific user need studies are described with their significant results. Interactions between users and an information system are described.
