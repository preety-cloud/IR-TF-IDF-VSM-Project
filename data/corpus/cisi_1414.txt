This book does not purport to provide all the answers to the problem (such a claim would be rightly suspect) but to collect together problems and experiences pertaining to the technique.  Though three of the case studies in Chapter 8 are from outside the United Kingdom, there is an inevitable bias towards British experience and English-language thesauri.  It is hoped that people outside this country will find the book useful, but there is little mention of, for example, the language problem.
