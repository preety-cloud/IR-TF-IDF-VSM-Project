Cataloguing in Publication:  A New Programme of Pre-Publication Cataloguing in the United States of America, with Comments on some Similar Programmes .A Clapp, Verner W. The cataloguing in source experiment carried out by the United States Library of Congress ended in 1959.. Studies on the feasibility of a new pre-publication cataloguing programme were undertaken and on 1 July 1971 a new cataloguing in publication programmes were started..
