ISDS and the Functions and Activities of National Centres .A Koster, C.J. An International Serials Data System (ISDS) is being established within the framework of UNISIST - a world science information system.  Each serial will be assigned an International Standard Serial Number (ISSN).  An International Centre for the Registration of Serial Publications, with headquarters in Paris, will be responsible for specifying the characteristics of the world register and maintaining an up-to-date file of serial titles.
