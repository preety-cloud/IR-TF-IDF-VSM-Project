Information Flow in Research and Development Laboratories .A Allen, Thomas J. Cohen, Stephen I. Technical communication patterns in two research and development laboratories were examined using modified sociometric techniques.. The structure of technical communication networks in the two laboratories results from the interaction of both social relations and work structure..
