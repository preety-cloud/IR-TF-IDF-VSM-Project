Automatic information, organization and retrieval .A Salton, G. This book deals with the computer processing of large information files, with special emphasis on automatic text handling methods. Described in particular are procedures for dictionary construction and dictionary look-up, statistical and syntactic language analysis methods, information search and matching procedures, automatic information dissemination systems, and methods for user interaction with the mechanized system.
