World's Chemical Literature Continues to Expand .A Baker, D.B. Abstracts of scientific papers from journals and other serial publications, which now make up about 85% of the abstracts in CA, increased at an average annual rate of 8.4% between 1961 and 1970, compared with 8.2% in the previous decade.  The number of such abstracts published annually now is doubling every 9 years.  The number of patent abstracts increased at an average rate of 5% per year in the 1960's compared with 11.7% in the 1950's.
