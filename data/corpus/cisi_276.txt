The Title Catalog: A Third Dimension .A Nitecki, Joseph Z. The accessibility of the card catalog seems to be inversely proportional to the complexity of its arrangement.. A catalog divided into author-title and subject sequence simplifies the filing order of cards and facilitates the use of each catalog.. It is argued here that a three-way division into author, title, and subject catalogs will further augment these advantages..
