An Approach to Performance Budgeting at the Florida Atlantic University Library .A Axford, H. William The article summarizes the problems encountered at the FAU library in the 1967 and the library's subsequent reorganization.. A detailed cost study is analyzed and the Clapp-Jordan and University of Washington formulae for budgeting are described, as well as a modified formula..
