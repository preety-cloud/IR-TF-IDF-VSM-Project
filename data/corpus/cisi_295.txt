An Approach to the Measurement of Use and Cost of a Large Academic Research Library System: A Report of a Study Done at Columbia University Libraries .A Mount, Ellis Fasana, Paul A description of the methodology used in collecting performance data in a large academic research library is given.. Twelve types of surveys used to measure and evaluate users, services, and materials were developed and conducted during the period 1968/69 at Columbia University libraries and later evaluated.. Sample results are included..
