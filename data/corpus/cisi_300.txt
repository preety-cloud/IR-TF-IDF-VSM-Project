Faculty Awareness and Attitudes Toward Academic Library Reference Services: A Measure of Communication .A Nelson, Jerold A survey of the faculties at six colleges was undertaken to measure the degree to which the libraries of those institutions were communicating with the faculty concerning the availability of various references services.. The results demonstrated that the average faculty member was aware of barely half the services actually available..
