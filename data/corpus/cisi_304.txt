The Management Review and Analysis Program: An Assisted Self-Study to Secure Constructive Change in the Management of Research Libraries .A Webster, D. E. The Management Review and Analysis Program (MRAP), designed, tested, and operated by the Association of Research Libraries' Office of University Library Management Studies (OMS), is an assisted self-study strategy intended for use by large academic and research libraries..
