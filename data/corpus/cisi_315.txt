Automatic Abstracting and Indexing - Survey and Recommendations .A Edmundson, H.P. .A Wyllys, R.E. In preparation for the widespread use of automatic scanner which will read documents and transmit their contents in automatic analysis: the relative- frequency approach to measuring the significance of words, word groups, and sentences.. The relative-frequency approach is discussed in detail, as is its application to problems of automatic indexing and automatic abstracting.. Included in the report is a summary of automatic analysis studies published as of the date of writing..
