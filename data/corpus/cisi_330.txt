Computer Readable Bibliographic Data Bases -- A Directory and Data Sourcebook .A Williams, M.E. This document, Computer Readable Bibliographic Data Bases -- A Directory and Data Sourcebook not only updates the 1973 publication, which included information on 81 data bases, but expands the coverage to 300 data bases and includes considerably more information about the data bases. Methodology used to obtain data involved:  (1) consulting published and unpublished sources; (2) design of a 13 page questionnaire; (3) mail survey; and (4) telephone follow up and verification.
