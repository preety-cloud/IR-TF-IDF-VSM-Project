CAS computer-Based Information Services .A Davenport, W.C. This paper describes the key points that characterize CAS' approach to large scale scientific-technical information systems that must handle over 100,000 documents per year. The first of these points is the "single analysis/multiple use" concept.  Intellectual ability is a rare commodity and essential to an information service.
