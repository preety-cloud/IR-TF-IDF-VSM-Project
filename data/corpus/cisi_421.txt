A Business Intelligence System .A Luhn, H.P. An automatic system is being developed to disseminate information to the various sections of any industrial, scientific or government organization.. This intelligence system will utilize data-processing machines for auto- abstracting and auto-encoding of documents and for creating interest profiles for each of the "action points" in an organization.. Both incoming and internally generated documents are automatically abstracted, characterized by a word pattern, and sent automatically to appropriate action points..
