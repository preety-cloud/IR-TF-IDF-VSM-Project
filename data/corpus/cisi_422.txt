On Some Clustering Techniques .A Bonner, R.E. The problem of organizing a large mass of data occurs frequently in research.. Normally, some process of generalization is used to compress the data so that it can be analyzed more easily.. A primitive step in this process is the "clustering" technique, which involves gathering together similar data into a cluster to permit a significant generalization.. This paper describes a number of methods which make use of IBM 7090 computer programs to do clustering..
