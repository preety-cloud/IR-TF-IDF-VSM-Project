Selection of Equifrequent Word Fragments for Information Retrieval .A Schuegraf, E. J. Heaps, H. S. The design of programs to research large document data bases is discussed with regard to the use of compression coding combined with adoption of word fragments as the basic language elements.. An algorithm is described for determination of a set of almost equifrequent fragments.. Its efficiency is tested for a sample data base formed from the MARC tapes.. A certain threshold frequency acts as a parameter whose value determines the number of distinct fragments..
