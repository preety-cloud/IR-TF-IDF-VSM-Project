Information in 1985; a forecasting study of information needs and resources .A Anderla, G. The primary purpose of this study is to estimate the supply of, and above all the demand for, scientific and technical information; its secondary objective is to make a long-term assessment of qualitative and quantitative requirements for information specialists. In both instances, of course, future technical developments, as far as they can be foreseen, must be taken into account.
