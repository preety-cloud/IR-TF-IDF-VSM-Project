Experimental Use of a Program for Computer-Aided Subject-Index Production .A Armitage, J.E. Lynch, M. F. Petrie, J. H. Belton, M. Stages in the development of a program for the production of articulated subject indexes are described.. The preparation of an index to the third edition of the SMRE Bibliography has enabled the technique to be assessed in a practical situation.. Titles from 1700 documents included in the bibliography were edited to provide input to the program, and the output recorded on a micro-film/hard-copy recorder in upper case alone..
