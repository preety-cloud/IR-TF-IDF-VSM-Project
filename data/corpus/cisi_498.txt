Mechanized Searching of Acts of Parliament .A Niblett, G.B.F. Price, N.H. This paper reports the results of a practical study of the application of digital computers to the storage, analysis and retrieval of the full text of Acts of Parliament and associated delegated legislation dealing with automatic energy, a total of some 140,000 words.. A suite of computer programs has been prepared in a high-level language for generating a dictionary and concordance to the full text and organizing the search process..
