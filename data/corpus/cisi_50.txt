Comparison of the Results of Bibliographic Coupling and Analytic Subject Indexing .A Kessler, M.M. A detailed comparison of how 334 papers in Volume 112 of Physical Review form related groups according to two criteria of relatedness.. The criteria are: (1) the Analytic Subject Index as used by the editors of the Physical Review; and (2) the method of Bibliographic Coupling..
