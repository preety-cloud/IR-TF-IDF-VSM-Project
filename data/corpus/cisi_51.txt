An Experiment in Automatic Indexing .A Damerau, Fred J. This report describes a method of indexing documents which is based on the assumptions, (1) that a subset of the words in a document can be an effective index to that document, and (2) that this subject can be approximated by selecting those words from the document whose frequencies are statistically unexpectedly high..
