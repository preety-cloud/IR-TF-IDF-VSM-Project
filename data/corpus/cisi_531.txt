Index Term Weighting .A Jones, Karen Sparck Various approaches to index term weighting have been investigated.. In particular, claims have been made for the value of statistically-based indexing in automatic retrieval systems.. The paper discusses the logic of different types of weighting, and describes experiments testing weighting schemes of these types..
