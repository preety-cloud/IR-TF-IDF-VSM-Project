Ascertaining Activities in a Subject Area Through Bibliometric Analysis .A Saracevic, Tefko Perk, Lawrence J. A combination of quantitative and qualitative analyses were used on the journal articles indexed in one volume of Library Literature.. Findings include: the dispersion of a articles among journals followed a Bradford-type distribution except for a "collapse" at the end, possibly showing low level of interaction of librarianship with other fields; considerable proportion of articles was of news-type; administration was the largest single subject covered..
