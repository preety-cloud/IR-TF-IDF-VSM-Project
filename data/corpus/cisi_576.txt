Classification of Scientific Documents by Means of Self_Generated Groups Employing Free Language .A Feinman, R. D. .A Kwok, K. L. A study was undertaken to classify mechanically a document collection using the free-language words in the titles and abstracts of a corpus of 261 physics research papers.. Using a clustering algorithm, results were obtained which closely duplicated the clusters obtained by previous experiments with citations.. A brief comparison is made with a traditional manual classification system..
