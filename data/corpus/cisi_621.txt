Dialectical Inquiring Systems: A New Methodology for Information Science .A Mitroff, Ian I. Williams, James Rathswohl, Eugene A taxonomy for modeling information systems is presented.. The taxonomy derives from the history of modern epistemology.. Epistemology is used as a prescriptive modeling basis because (1) the fundamental problems of epistemology are concerned with information, and (2), because the history of epistemology identifies any archetypal ways that men have conceived of information and of models.. It is shown how the different models of epistemology can be made scientifically operational through the use of a special kind of systems analysis..
