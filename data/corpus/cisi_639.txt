Costs and Effectiveness in the Evolution of an Information System: A Case Study .A Standera, Oldrich R. With budget constraints, the problems of cost and effectiveness have advanced to the top of priority list of all information services managers.. Determining and monitoring effectiveness should be cost-effective also.. The author traces the cost-effectiveness problems back to the genesis of an information system.. Performance parameters and their shifting in the desired direction are briefly discussed.. The monthly statement is intended to assist a manager in budgeting and planning and it should also alert him to any irregularities in costs and effectiveness, either in time or among individual services..
