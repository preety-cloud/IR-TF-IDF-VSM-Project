Creation and Use of Citation Data Bases: A Modest Proposal .A Waters, Samuel T. Improvement in the production and use of citations to research literature calls for coordination of activities by a number of abstracting and indexing services.. The Ohio College Library Center (OCLC) has developed a cost-effective on-line shared cataloging system, now in widespread use by U.S. libraries.. It could readily serve as a pattern for a system to create and share descriptive indexing on-line..
