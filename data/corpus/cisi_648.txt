User Training for On-Line Information Retrieval Systems .A Moghdam, Dineh The early 1970's have clearly shown a trend toward the use of on-line systems as the ideal medium for information retrieval.. The emphasis placed on direct access by the practitioners in the field, rather than delegated searches through information specialists, leads to the growing need for an efficient design in training transient user groups.. Printed manuals, live help, audiovisual presentations and on-line instructions have all been used with varying degrees of success..
