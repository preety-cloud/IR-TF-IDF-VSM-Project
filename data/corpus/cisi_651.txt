An Acquisitions Decision Model for Academic Libraries .A DePew, John N. A decision model for book acquisitions has been developed to simulate the intellectual processes used in acquiring these materials in academic libraries.. It consists of a flow chart, weighted inputs and an equation, which when solved indicates whether a library should add the title to its collection, refer it to a cooperative group, defer the decision or drop it altogether..
