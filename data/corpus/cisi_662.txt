Automatic Indexing: An Experimental Inquiry .A Maron, M. E. This inquiry examines a technique for automatically classifying (indexing) documents according to their subject content.. The task, in essence, is to have a computing machine read a document and on the basis of the occurrence of selected clue words decide to which of many subject categories the document in question belongs..
