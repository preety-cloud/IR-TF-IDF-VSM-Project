A Mathematical Method for Analyzing the Growth of a Scientific Discipline .A Goffman, W. The spread of ideas within a scientific community and the spread of infectious disease are both special cases of a general communication process.  Thus a general theory of epidemics can explain the growth of symbolic logic from 1847 to 1962.  An epidemic model predicts the rise and fall of particular research areas within symbolic logic.  A Markov chain model of individual movement between research areas indicates that once an individual leaves an area he is not expected to return.
