ISI's Experiences with ASCA - A Selective Dissemination System .A Garfield, E. Sher, I.H. ASCA (Automatic Subject Citation Alert) is a commercially available SDI system covering the journal literature.  The repertoire of questions which ASCA can utilize includes cited references, words from titles, authors, organizations, etc., and allows for logical combinations of these questions. This paper discusses differences and similarities between "citations" and "words" in retrieving and disseminating information.  The problem of user- system interaction is explored, and some techniques for developing effective interest profiles are described.
