A Correlative Notation System for NMR Data .A Skolnik, Herman A new linear notation system which denotes carbon in terms of bounds and attached hydrogen(s) is used to correlate proton groups in organic molecules with chemical shifts.. The notation system is illustrated with acyclic and cyclic examples, and the production of tables of NMR data via computer by proton group vis-a-vis neighboring groups is demonstrated..
