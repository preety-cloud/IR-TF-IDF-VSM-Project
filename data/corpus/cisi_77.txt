The Effectiveness of Automatically Generated Weights and Links in Mechanical Indexing .A Artandi, Susan .A Wolf, Edward H. Work concerned with the statistical evaluation of the output of the MEDICO automatic indexing method is described.. The statistical test were designed primarily to examine the validity of the assumptions which formed the basis of the algorithms developed for the automatic computation of weights and for the automatic generation of links between index terms and modifiers..
