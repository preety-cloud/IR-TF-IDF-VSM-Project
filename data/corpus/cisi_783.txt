Author Versus Title: A Comparative Survey of the Accuracy of the Information Which the User Brings to the Library Catalogue .A Ayres, F. H. .A German, Janice .A Loukes, N. .A Searle, R. H. Details are given of a survey carried out in a large scientific special library on the comparative accuracy of the author and title information which the user brings to the catalogue.. The sample was restricted to requests for book material.. The results are analyzed in detail and show the title to be more accurate..
