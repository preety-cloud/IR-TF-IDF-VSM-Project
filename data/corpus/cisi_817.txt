The Aberrystwyth Index Languages Test .A Keen, Michael E. Reports a laboratory comparision of the effectiveness and efficiency of five index languages in the subject area of library and information science; three post-co-ordinate languages, Compressed Term, Uncontrolled, and Hierarchically Structured, and two pre-co-ordinate ones, Hierarchically Structures and Relational Indexing.. Eight test comparisons were made, and factors studied were index language specificity and linkage, indexing specificity and exhaustivity, method of co-ordination, the precision devices of partitioning and relational operators, and the provision of context in the search file..
