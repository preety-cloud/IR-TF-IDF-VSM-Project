Foundation of Evaluation .A Van Rijsbergen, C.J. The mathematical theory of measurement is used to construct a framework for the evaluation of information retrieval strategies.  The model arrived at is based on precision and recall.  After a detailed examination of the kind of conditions the model can be expected to satisfy a plausible measure of effectiveness is derived.
