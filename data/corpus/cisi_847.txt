Library Instruction and the Development of the Individual .A Hills, P. J. The purpose of a university education is defined here as the need to guide the student towards becoming a self-directing individual.. This paper examines this purpose in terms of student needs and the awareness of both academic and library staff.. Psychological principles of learning as applied to this purpose are considered together with the use of audio-visual methods..
