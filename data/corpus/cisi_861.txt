The RECON Pilot Project: A Progress Report November 1969 - April 1970 .A Avram, Henriette D. Guiles, Kay D. Maruyama, Lenore S. A synthesis of the second progress report submitted by the Library of Congress to the Council on Library Resources under a grant for the RECON Pilot Project.. An overview of the progress made from November 1969 to April 1970 in the following areas: production,Official Catalog comparison,format recognition, research titles, microfilming, investigation of input devices..
