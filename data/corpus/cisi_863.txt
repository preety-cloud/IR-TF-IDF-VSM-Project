RECON Pilot Project:  A Progress Report, April-September 1970 .A Avram, Henriette D Maruyama, Lenore S. A synopsis of the third progress report on the RECON Pilot Project submitted by the Library of Congress to the Council on Library Resources.. An overview is given of the progress made from April through September 1970 in the following areas:  RECON production, format recognition, research titles, microfilming, and investigation of input devices.
