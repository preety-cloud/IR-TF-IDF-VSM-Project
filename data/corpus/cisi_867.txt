An Algorithm for Compaction of Alphanumeric Data .A Schieber, William D. Thomas, George W. Description of a technique for compressing data to be placed in computer auxiliary storage.. The technique operates on the principle of taking two alphabetic characters frequently used in combination and replacing them with one unused special character code..
