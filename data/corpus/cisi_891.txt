The Status and Needs of Medical School Libraries in the United States .A Bloomquist, H. This paper, prepared at the request of the National Library of Medicine, is the result of a study of the status and needs of the libraries of 86 medical schools and schools of basic medical sciences which are institutional members of the Association of American Medical Libraries and are approved by the American Medical Association.
