The Georgia Tech Library's Microfiche Catalog .A Roberts, Edward G. Kennedy, John P. The Price Gilbert Memorial Library of the Georgia Institute of Technology has reproduced its entire card catalog on microfiche, and is updating this basic file with a bimonthly cumulated supplement produced by the Computer Output Microfiche (COM) process.. The total catalog has been placed, with in appropriate microfiche reader, in every academic and research department on campus - thereby taking the chief bibliographic record of the library to the faculty user..
