Shawnee Mission's On-Line Cataloging System .A Miller, Ellen W. Hodges, B. J. An on_line cataloging pilot project for two elementary school is discussed.. The system components are 2740 terminals, upper-lower-case input, IBM's FASTER generalized software package, and usual cards/labels output.. Reasons for choosing FASTER, software and hardware features, operating procedures, system performance and costs are detailed.
