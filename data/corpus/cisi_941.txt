ISBD Standard or Secret? .A Swanson, G. The controversial ISBD will mean radical changes in descriptive cataloging practice if put into operation, as planned, by the Library of Congress. Users of LC catalog cards will require retraining: all reference librarians will experience an immediate and continuing demand for explanation of the new catalog cards to users; those large public and research libraries with computer-based systems will require costly modifications of computer programs.
