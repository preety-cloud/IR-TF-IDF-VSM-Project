An Operations Research Study of a Variable Loan and Duplication Policy at the University of Lancaster .A Buckland, Michael K. The Library Research Unit of the University of Lancaster used an Operations Research (O.R.) approach to recommend changes in loan and duplication policies in the university library.. The "variable" loan and duplication policy which was developed is described and also the considerable impact of implementation.. Other libraries are now adopting this kind of policy.. The work is presented as a case study in library O.R. .. The great importance of analyzing the structure of problems is stressed and the nature and usefulness of models is described..
