An Analysis of the Universal Decimal Classification as a Term System for Nuclear Science and Technology .A Stueart, Robert D. Explores the possibilities of merging the terminology of the Universal Decimal Classification System with that of a term system - Engineers Point Council's Thesaurus - for nuclear science and technology.. Concludes, from the evidence presented, that UDC can be effectively used as a term system..
