Measuring Reader Failure at the Catalogue .A Seymour, Carol A. .A Schofield, J. L. In an effort to develop a simple method for librarians to employ to measure and evaluate author catalogue use, the Library Management Research Unit tested a survey design in four varying libraries.. The reader was asked to note details of items not found in the catalogue, the source of the reference, and his status.. The items were then checked by library staff to discover the cause of "failure"..
